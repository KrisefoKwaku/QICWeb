This policy is designed to pay compensation to you for Loss of/or Damage to physical assets owned by you. The perils covered are itemized as follows:

What we cover

Fire

Lightening

Explosion

Electrical installation

Water tanks or storage

Flood

Riot, strike, civil commotion

Aircraft damage

Impact by land moving vehicle or animal

The burglary insurance will be arranged to compensate you for loss of or damage to any property covered under the policy as a result of burglary or attempted burglary, accompanied by actual forcible or violent breaking into or exit from any of the premises in which the insured property is contained.

Cover will also extend to include damage to premises as a result of the burglary.
