ARE YOU PROTECTED? Amidst life’s uncertainties, you can still enjoy to the fullest

By Staff Writer
Scroll

Life is good

Many people are reluctant to take insurance policies mainly due to ignorance or lack of confidence in the industry. That may explain why Ghana has a low insurance penetration rate. Whether it is an issue of trust or no education, insurance companies have their work cut out but everyone has the responsibility of protecting themselves and/or their business entities. There are currently about 51 insurance companies covering life and non-life portfolios making various efforts to educate the public on the need for insurance and to increase their client base. Contrary to widely held perceptions, insurance companies are not the sole beneficiaries of policies they sell, the insured also benefits, hence, it is mutually beneficial.

Considering the uncertainty of life as well as the risks associated with it, it is very important that individuals and institutions put in place necessary measures to mitigate these occurrences. One of the ways to do this is by taking up the various policies offered by the Quality Insurance Company (QIC). Your protection will be solid as Gold!

‍Insurance is a tool primarily used to hedge against the risk of a contingent or uncertain loss, thus providing financial security. Insurance policies are tailored to suit various needs. Depending on your need, you can easily get an insurance policy be it on life or property. It is obvious that at different stages of life, people/institutions will have various insurance needs and therefore it is necessary that everyone factors insurance into planning their life or business entity.

Without an insurance cover, unexpected events can set an individual or a business entity back no matter what measures have been put in place. Regardless of your preparedness, a fire outbreak that wipes away a home or business will surely set you or your business back. Insurance policies are very accessible and affordable these days; apart from going directly to any QIC office, you can purchase your insurance through brokers, agents and even online. Everyone has to be mindful of the fact that life is full of risks and take necessary steps to ensure they are never thrown out of balance when the unfortunate happens. The solution is readily available so take advantage of it; an insurance cover is a must have TODAY.
