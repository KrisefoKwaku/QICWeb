GoGirl for the Savvy
Woman of Today

As part of our efforts to provide insurance education and competitive products and services, we are pleased to introduce to you the “GO GIRL COMPREHENSIVE MOTOR INSURANCE POLICY’’ specifically designed for women. This product won the CIIG Insurance Product of the year in 2019 and Product Innovation Award in 2020 by the Ghana Insurance Awards.

GO GIRL was born out of our flagship policy, Kingly Queenly Motor Comprehensive Policy, where the courtesy car is a paid benefit.

Over the years, we have realized that majority of our KQ policy holders have been young professional women. Therefore, QIC decided to develop the Go Girl policy to reward these professional women, where women who sign up for this policy enjoy certain benefits on their comprehensive motor insurance at no extra cost.

As part of our corporate social responsibility, a percentage of all Go Girl premiums goes to help support the building of a Childhood Cancer Center at the Korle Bu Teaching hospital.

Enjoy free benefits when you sign up:

Provision of a free courtesy car at the time of claim
One-time car servicing
Facilitation of the two (2) year mandatory driver’s license renewal at the Prestige Center (DVLA)
As part of our corporate social responsibility, a percentage of all Go Girl premiums goes to help support the building of a Childhood Cancer Center at the Korle Bu Teaching hospital
