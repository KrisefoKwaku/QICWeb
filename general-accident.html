Cover options

Fidelity Guarantee

The QIC Fidelity Guarantee Policy compensates the employer against loss of monies as a result of the dishonesty of employee(s) who handle cash and large stock of goods. Our policy may be issued as a blanket policy that names relevant positions instead of named employee(s).

Bankers Indemnity

This type of insurance provides comprehensive insurance coverage from one single insurance policy to institutions which have heavy financial transactions like Banks.

Scope

The risks covered under this insurance are Cash/Valuables in the premises, Cash/Valuables in Transit, Forgery, Alteration, Infidel activities of staffs etc.

Goods-in-transit

The policy compensates for loss of or damage to goods being transported from one destination to another. The risk cover includes loss as a result of Road Traffic Accident, Fire Out Break and Robbery. Extra Risks such as Extortion, Collusion with Insured’s staff and theft can be endorsed on the policy. Additionally, the risk of loss or damage while goods are being loaded or off loaded can also be insured.

Money

Our Money Insurance Policy provides an all risk cover for money:

  • In Transit within and outside the Premises
  • Cash in Locked Safes
  • Loss of/or damage to Safes and Strong room, Cash-Boxes etc.

Burglary

QIC realizing the effect of burglary on businesses designed this policy to compensate for theft involving entry to and exit from premises by forcible and violent means resulting in the loss of property to the insured. Under the policy, we will provide financial compensation for any loss incurred as a result of the burglary. Insurance on stock could be arranged on first loss. The premium is charged on the value of contents.

Group Personal Accident

The Group Personal Accident Policy is designed to cover bodily injury and/or death to the insured i.e. workers for accident at workplace, home or at leisure hours. It is mostly designed for senior staff and above as part of an incentive package.
